Store Management System

The Store Management System Project is a command-line interface (CLI) application designed to streamline operations for shopkeepers and enhance the shopping experience for customers. Shopkeepers can efficiently track their products and sales, manage inventory, and analyze sales data. Customers can easily calculate their bills by selecting products and specifying quantities. With its user-friendly interface and comprehensive features, this system simplifies store management tasks while ensuring a seamless and convenient shopping process for customers.
